The Game

In Dust 2 Dust, you are a spirit summoner venturing into mysterious and perilous lands. Your weapons are spirits — magical orbs that channel elemental attacks, shields, and healing energy.

Build a powerful deck of spirits, master elemental synergies, and push deeper into increasingly dangerous territory. Every action you take is verified on-chain, making your progress permanent and provably fair.

Combat

Your Deck

Before each battle, you choose 7 spirits from your collection to bring into combat. Each round, 3 of your 7 spirits are drawn — the selection rotates every turn, so plan your deck carefully.

Each round, 3 spirits are drawn (highlighted). The rest wait for future rounds.

Battle Rounds

Each round follows this sequence:

  1. Draw spirits — 3 of your 7 spirits become available
  2. Choose targets — assign each spirit to an enemy (or all enemies)
  3. Abilities resolve — your spirits deal damage, block, or heal
  4. Energy combos trigger — matching energy colors unlock bonus effects
  5. Enemies fight back — they attack, block, and heal

Defeat all enemies before your health reaches zero. Damage accumulates across rounds, so use your block and heal spirits wisely.

Damage Types & Effectiveness

There are three attack elements. Each enemy has different resistances — choosing the right element is key to dealing maximum damage.

Energy Combos

Some spirits generate colored energy when used. If another spirit in the same round shares that energy color, a bonus effect triggers — extra damage, additional healing, or stronger shields.

Building a deck with matching energy colors creates powerful synergies that can turn the tide of battle.

Your Main Goal

Quests & Bosses

Quests are the heart of Dust 2 Dust. This is how you progress through the game, unlock new regions, and face the most powerful enemies.

How Quests Work

Unlike regular battles, quests are asynchronous expeditions that culminate in a boss encounter. Send your spirits out, and return when they're ready.

Pick a Region
Choose 7 Spirits
Send on Quest
Wait...
Fight the Boss

After sending your spirits on a quest, you can continue battling or managing your collection. When the quest timer completes, return to finalize the quest and face the region's boss.

Why Quests Matter

Defeating a boss unlocks the next difficulty tier for that region, bringing tougher enemies — and better rewards. To reach the endgame, you must conquer every region at every difficulty.

Regular battles are great for farming gold and spirits, but only quests advance your progression. Build your deck through battles, then put it to the ultimate test against a boss.

The Shop

Upgrade Spirits

Sacrifice one spirit and pay gold to empower another. Each upgrade level adds 30% more power. A fully upgraded spirit with 3 stars is a formidable force.

Sell Spirits

Unwanted spirits can be sold for gold. Stronger spirits are worth more — use this to fund your upgrades.

Gold Economy

Gold is earned from battles and selling spirits. Spend it wisely — the difference between victory and defeat often comes down to a well-timed upgrade.
